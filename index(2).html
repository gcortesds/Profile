What to expect

I work best with defined scope and genuine autonomy inside it. Once I understand the context and the rationale, I require minimal oversight to deliver — and I deliver above what is asked for.

I am direct, precise, and flag problems early. I do not soften bad news beyond what clarity requires. I think before I speak, write carefully, and hold positions that are reasoned.

I am most effective in roles combining genuine complexity, real ownership of outcomes, and work that matters. I am least effective in high-volume processing roles or environments where visibility is rewarded over output.
